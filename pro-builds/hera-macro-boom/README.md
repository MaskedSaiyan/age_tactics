# Hera — Defensive Macro Boom

### Build Guide · *copy the world champion*

Reverse-engineered from a real **VIT | Hera** game (multiple-time AoE2 world champion) vs
*"if i lose it all"* — a 52-minute 1v1. This is the build, laid out to follow step by step. Drill
it until it's automatic. The single number that makes it work, and the one you have to beat:

> **Hera's Town Center idle time, whole game: `00:00`.** Yours last game: `14:45`.
> Copy *that* before anything else — the villager queue is **never** empty.

> **Villager numbers** are the population count *after* that villager pops. Drop a **House**
> before 5/10/15/20 pop — never get housed. The building/age **times** are read straight from
> Hera's replay (use them as checkpoints). Resource splits are the standard ones for this build —
> the replay records every building, unit and click exactly, but not which villager walked to
> which pile, so the gather assignments are the proven ones for this boom.

## Pros & Cons

**✅ Pros**
- **The most transferable habit in the game** — "TC never idle" works on every civ, map, and matchup.
- Low-risk: a Feudal that *defends* instead of all-ins, so you rarely die to your own greed.
- Pike + Skirmisher trash costs almost no gold — a fat eco turns straight into an unkillable ball.

**❌ Cons**
- **Boring to do well.** No flashy moment — the discipline *is* the build.
- Needs walls / map control to survive the long Feudal; if you can't hold, the greed punishes you.
- It's a grind plan. If you need a fast knockout, this isn't it.

## Build Order

### ☀️ Dark Age — *to Feudal (~6:41 click)*

**`3` Sheep** — 3 starting Villagers onto one sheep under the TC. **Queue Villagers non-stop and never stop until the game ends.** ◄ *this line is the whole build*

**`4` Sheep** — **House**, then sheep.

**`5` Sheep** — **House**, then sheep.

**`6` Berries** — Build a **Mill** on berries (~1:56) and gather. *(early food so the TC never waits on a villager's cost)*

**`7` Boar** — **Lure the boar** under the TC, eat it.

**`8–11` Food** — Sheep / boar / berries, whatever's closest. Keep food *ahead* of the queue.

**`12` Boar** — **Lure the second boar.** *(free food = idle insurance)*

**`13–16` Food** — Push **deer** with a couple of vils if they're there; otherwise sheep/berries.

**`17–21` Wood** — Send the next villagers to wood; build a **Lumber Camp** (~5:56) to fund Feudal + a Barracks.

**`22` Food/Wood** — Balance to afford Feudal.

> **⏫ ▶ Click Feudal at ~22 pop (~6:41).** Aim for **idle 0** more than the exact time.

### ⚔️ Feudal Age — *defend cheap, boom hard*

**`Barracks` (~8:00)** — drop it as you arrive, and trickle just **4 Spearmen** across the whole age (Hera made them ~9:43 / 10:24 / 12:59 / 13:50). **Minimum army to not die — everything else is eco.**

**`Farms` (start ~10:20)** — transition off sheep onto farms *without a beat of TC idle*. Add a **2nd Mill** if your berries/food camp is dry.

**`Watch Tower` ×2 (~12:38)** + **`Mining Camp` (~12:49)** — towers hold the raids while you boom; mining opens gold/stone for Castle.

**`Market` (~14:58) + `Blacksmith` (~16:01)** — trade to smooth the eco; Blacksmith so the defensive army bites.

> Feudal scorecard: **~4 military units in 10 minutes.** Almost the entire age on villagers and farms. *Safe* greed.

### 🏰 Castle Age — *the boom detonates (click ~16:46, ~40 vils)*

- **Keep every TC producing.** Climb 40 → ~139 villagers by Imperial. This is where everyone else lets the TC cool — you don't.
- **`Monastery` (~23:54)** for a Monk or two; a **`Castle` (~28:54)** as a defensive anchor.
- Wall, hold, add **Pikemen + the first Skirmishers** as the enemy's army demands.
- Idle still **`00:00`.**

### 👑 Imperial Age — *bury them (click ~29:44, ~139 vils)*

- **Trash core:** mass the **Spear → Pike line (Hera ended on ~281)** + **~34 Skirmishers** — a gold-free wall that beats cavalry *and* archers.
- **Gold punch:** add your main gold unit in bulk (Hera massed ~65 from ~37:36), backed by an **`Archery Range` (~30:47)** and **`Siege Workshop` (~33:42)**.
- 190 villagers means you **re-make a dead army faster than they can kill it.** That's the win — the eco behind the fight, not the fight.

## Tips

- **Drill the queue, not the clock.** Put a villager in the TC the instant the last one pops — make it muscle memory *first*, hit 6:41 later.
- **Both boars + deer = idle insurance.** Hera's early Mill + food focus is *why* the TC never waited. Lure aggressively.
- **4 Spearmen held a world-class player in Feudal.** You don't need a Feudal army — you need to *not die* while the eco runs.
- **Trash wins long games.** Pike + Skirm costs no gold; a big eco makes it endless. Save gold for the punch units.

## TL;DR

**Villagers non-stop (idle = 0) → Mill on berries ~1:56, both boars → Lumber Camp ~5:56 → Feudal
~6:41 → Barracks 8:00 + 4 Spearmen + 2 Watch Towers to *not die* → farm boom → Castle 16:46, never
stop the TC → Imperial 29:44 → mass Pike + Skirmisher + a gold punch.** 90% of this build is "the
TC queue is never empty." Go beat your 14:45.

# Goths — Men-at-Arms → Archers

### Build Guide · *the committed feudal grind*

If the [`../maa-rush/`](../maa-rush/) poke is landing — his vils are running, he's walling — this
is how you **commit and grind him out in Feudal** instead of backing off. Men-at-Arms eat the
eco, Archers add ranged damage, and Spears/Skirms hard-counter whatever he answers with. This is
the build that **flips the matchup**: he's used to pressuring you, here you never give him the turn.

## Pros & Cons

**✅ Pros**
- Cheap Goth infantry means MAA **and** Spears barely dent your eco — you out-mass his army.
- Full answer to shura's playbook: Spears > his Scouts, Skirms > his Archers, MAA > his vils.
- Takes the initiative. **You** dictate the game instead of reacting.

**❌ Cons**
- Juggling 3 unit types in Feudal is real micro — harder than the pure poke.
- Two military buildings + units **delays your Castle time** — you must convert the pressure.
- If you grind with no damage, you just fall behind. Commit only when the poke is *working*.

## Build Order

### ☀️ Dark Age — *to Feudal ~9:45*

Identical opener to the [`../maa-rush/`](../maa-rush/) — get it from there:

**`3→6` Food → `Lumber Camp` ~2:00 → lure both boars → `Mill` ~4:20 → vils non-stop → `Barracks` ~8:00 → `3` Militia → click Feudal ~9:45 at ~18 pop.**

The only change: keep **4–5 on wood** (not 3). You're about to build an **Archery Range** and pump arrows — that costs wood.

### ⚔️ Feudal Age — *the grind*

**Research `Man-at-Arms`** immediately. Walk the 3 in and start eating villagers.

**`Archery Range`** (~10:30) — build it as soon as you're up. Start pumping **Archers** behind the MAA.
> MAA force the enemy to fight; Archers melt them from behind. This combo is the damage core.

**`Blacksmith` → Fletching / Forging** — turn the volume up on both.

**React to his army** (this is the whole point):
- **He has Scouts** → **`Spearmen`**. Cheap, hard-counter cavalry — his raids die on them.
- **He adds Archers** → **`Skirmishers`**. They shut archers down. Or be heading to Castle.

**`2–3` Farms** start the food transition; keep the TC working.

### 🏰 Convert or climb (~17–19 min)

- **Eco crippled?** Keep grinding, add a 2nd Range/Barracks, and look for the kill.
- **Even-ish?** You've done your damage — **click Castle** *ahead* on eco and take the game there.

Don't grind forever with no payoff. Goths spike late; a Feudal lead you bank into Castle is a win.

---

## Tips

- **MAA are the anvil, Archers are the hammer.** Hold his army with Men-at-Arms, kill it with arrows.
- **Spears are cheap insurance**, not your main army — 4–6 is plenty to neuter Scouts.
- **Don't get housed and don't let the TC idle** — the rush eco is tight, every gap hurts.
- **No Scouts of your own.** Goths have no cavalry bonus; your aggression is feet, not hooves.

## TL;DR

**MAA poke → commit with an Archery Range → Spears for his Scouts, Skirms for his Archers →
grind his eco → convert to a kill or a Castle lead. Take the turn and never give it back.**

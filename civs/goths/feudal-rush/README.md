# Goths — Feudal infantry flush / Men-at-Arms rush

A simple, low-commitment Feudal pressure build. The point is **not** to win in Feudal —
Goths don't have the tools for that. The point is to **create idle time and disruption** on an
enemy while you transition to your real game plan (boom → Castle → Imperial).

## The plan

1. **3 Militia before clicking Feudal.**
   - Queue Militia out of the Barracks while you're finishing your Dark Age eco.
   - Cheap because of the Goth infantry discount — you barely feel it.
2. **Upgrade to Men-at-Arms** the moment you hit Feudal.
   - Now they actually hurt: harass villagers, kill stragglers, force the enemy to react.
3. **Walk them into the enemy eco** and make a nuisance of yourself.

## Goal: idle time and disruption, not a kill

- You're trying to make the **enemy villagers run**, force them to wall, and pull them off
  resources. Every second their TC is idle or their vils are running is value.
- If you snipe a couple of villagers, great — but don't suicide your Men-at-Arms into a TC or
  spears for one kill. **Disruption > trades.**
- Even a "failed" rush that buys you map space and a slower enemy Castle time is a success.

## Transition out

Goths fall off in Feudal, so don't overstay. Smoothly transition into:

- **Walls** — protect your own eco so you can boom safely.
- **Farms** — get your food economy rolling for the Castle Age boom.
- **Castle Age** — your real timing. Head toward the 3-TC boom and eventually Imperial
  (see [`../fast-castle/`](../fast-castle/) and [`../team-imperial/`](../team-imperial/)).

The Men-at-Arms can keep poking or peel back to defend while you set up.

## ⚠️ Warning: do not overmake Scouts with Goths

- Goths have **no cavalry bonuses** — your Scouts are just vanilla Scouts, and every Scout is
  food + a unit that doesn't synergize with your late game.
- Over-investing in Scouts delays your boom and your Castle time, which is exactly what Goths
  can't afford. Your power is **infantry + numbers later**, not early cavalry pressure.
- A scout for map vision/scouting is fine. A *stable* of raiding scouts is a trap. If you want
  early aggression as Goths, it's **Men-at-Arms**, not Scouts.

## TL;DR

3 Militia → Men-at-Arms → harass for idle time and disruption → wall up, farm, head to Castle.
Don't go for kills, don't overstay, and don't waste food on Scouts.

## Pros & Cons

**✅ Pros**
- A forward Castle is a **production building + a fortress** — hard to remove, pumps your UU.
- **Huskarls** shred archers and **raze buildings** — perfect for cracking his base.
- Goth **infantry discount** means you flood Huskarls/infantry cheaper than anyone.

**❌ Cons**
- **Stone-hungry** — 650 stone for the Castle means a dedicated stone line, or no drop.
- Commits hard and forward — if the Castle dies cheaply, you're down a Castle's worth of eco.
- Weak if he has **siege of his own** (onagers/rams) to answer the Castle and Huskarl balls.

## Build Order

### ☀️ Dark Age → ⚔️ Feudal (~10:24)

**`3` Sheep** — start on sheep, **villagers non-stop.**

**`4–6` Food** — 2 Houses, lure the **boar**, keep food flowing.

**`7–9` Wood** — **Lumber Camp**; you need wood for farms + buildings.

**`10–13` Food / Stone** — **Mill** on berries, and **put ~3 on stone** (Mining Camp) — this is what funds the drop.

> **▶ Click Feudal ~10:24 at ~27 villagers.** Heavier than the rush — the eco funds the fast Castle + stone. Loom in, keep mining stone.

### 🏰 Feudal → Castle (~14:33)

- **Market + Blacksmith** on reaching Feudal — Market to buy/sell stone if you're short, Blacksmith for infantry upgrades.
- **Approaching Castle (~30 vils): STOP making villagers and shift food → stone.** That production pause *is* the castle drop — it's how you bank the **~650 stone** in time.
- Farms seeded so food doesn't gap during the push.

> **▶ Click Castle ~14:30 (<15 min) with stone ready.** *(This is real — your 2023 games hit 14:33.)*

### ⚔️🏰 The Drop (~18:00)

1. **Walk 3–4 villagers forward** toward his base as you age up.
2. **Plant the Castle ~18:00** — close enough to pressure, safe enough not to insta-die (near a hill / your forward woodline, not point-blank on his TC).
3. **Pump Huskarls** out of it immediately — they kill his archers and start razing buildings.
4. **Siege Workshop ~21:00** (Battering Rams / Mangonels) to crack his defensive buildings and TCs.
5. Back it with **cheap Militia/Spears** from a Barracks so the Huskarls aren't alone.

### 👑 Climb (~25:30)

Cheap army = you can **click Imperial ~25:30** while still pressuring. Elite Huskarl + Blast Furnace
turn the forward faucet into a base-eraser. Don't stop producing from the Castle.

## Tips

- **Stone is the whole build.** If you don't bank ~650 by Castle, there's no drop. Mine early.
- **Placement > aggression.** A Castle that survives and produces beats a Castle planted too greedily
  on his TC that dies in two minutes. Forward, but on safe ground.
- **Huskarls love archers and buildings** — point them at his ranged units and his base, not his Knights.
- **Watch his siege.** If he's making onagers/rams, spread your Huskarls and bring your own Mangonels.

# Goths

The classic late-game team-game **win condition**. Cheap infantry, an absurd unit cap on
Huskarls, and the fastest unit production in the game once you're rolling. Goths don't want
to win in 20 minutes — they want to drown you in bodies at minute 40.

## Strengths

- **Cheapest infantry in the game** — the discount compounds across the whole game.
- **Huskarls** that shred archers, buildings, and most trash. They ignore most pierce armor.
- **Perfusion** — barracks produce units *way* faster, so a few rax become an army faucet.
- **Insane population efficiency in trash wars** — cheap units + fast production = you replace
  losses faster than the enemy can.
- **+10 population cap** team bonus helps the *whole team* boom and fight bigger.

## Weaknesses

- **No good answer to skirmishers + siege** early/mid. Cheap infantry melt to massed skirms.
- **Weak in Feudal and early Castle** — your power spikes late. Before then you're fragile.
- **No cavalry, no reliable ranged answer.** You lean hard on Huskarls and numbers.
- **Map control is rough early** — you can't pressure like Scouts/Knights civs.
- **If the game ends early, you lose.** You *need* to reach the late game alive.

## Team bonus

**+10 population cap for the whole team.** That's not just for you — your pocket Knight player
and your archer flank both get more army. Lean into being the slot that wants the game to go long.

## Infantry discount

Goth infantry get a **cost discount that scales by age**:

- Dark Age: **-20%**
- Feudal Age: **-25%**
- Castle Age: **-30%**
- Imperial Age: **-35%**

By Imperial your Huskarls and Champions are stupidly cheap. This is *the* reason you can
out-produce and out-mass anyone in a trash war.

## Huskarls

- Infantry with **high pierce armor** — they laugh at archers, skirms, and Town Center fire.
- Great vs buildings, great vs archers, great vs most trash.
- **Counters:** heavy melee (Knights, Champions, Eagles), and especially **siege** (onagers,
  scorpions). Don't walk Huskarls into onager pancakes.
- Elite Huskarl upgrade in Imperial is a top-priority tech (see team-imperial notes).

## Perfusion

Goth unique tech (Imperial). **Barracks create infantry much faster.** Combined with the
infantry discount, a handful of barracks become a non-stop army faucet. This is the single
biggest reason Goths feel oppressive late: you don't out-tech the enemy, you **out-flow** them.

Build **a lot of barracks** in late Castle / early Imperial so Perfusion has something to work with.

## Why Goths are a late-game team-game win condition

Put it together: **+10 pop for the team, the cheapest army in the game, and the fastest
production once Perfusion lands.** In a stable team game that reaches Imperial, Goths become
the slot that converts a pile of food + the team's slung resources into an endless wave of
cheap, expendable units. You trade inefficiently on purpose, because you replace losses faster
than anyone. The plan is simple: **survive to Imperial, then never stop pumping.**

## ⚠️ Warning: don't over-boom with too many Town Centers

It's tempting to slam down 4-5 Town Centers and boom forever. **Don't.** Goths' strength is
*production from barracks*, not from Town Centers.

- Too many TCs = too much wood/stone spent on TCs and too many villagers, and you arrive at
  Imperial **late and broke** with no army.
- Each extra TC past 3 has diminishing returns *and* delays the units that actually win.
- **3 TCs is plenty** for a Goth boom. Spend the surplus on **barracks, upgrades, and army.**

The Goth failure mode isn't "too few villagers" — it's "I'm 105 pop in Castle Age with 4 TCs
and no military while the enemy hits Imperial." Get to Imperial, then let cheap units do the work.

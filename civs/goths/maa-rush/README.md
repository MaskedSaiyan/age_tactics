## Pros & Cons

**✅ Pros**
- Goth **infantry discount** (−20% Dark, −25% Feudal) makes the Militia/MAA almost free.
- Punishes greedy fast-eco players (exactly shura's style).
- Low skill-floor aggression: no micro-heavy kiting like Scouts/Archers.

**❌ Cons**
- Goths are weak in Feudal — overstay and you fall behind. This is a *poke*, not a kill.
- Walls + a couple of Spears shut it down; you must read when to peel off and boom.
- No Scout safety net — if the rush whiffs, your eco has to carry you to the late game.

## Build Order

### ☀️ Dark Age — *to Feudal ~9:45*

**`3` Sheep** — Send your 3 starting Villagers onto one sheep under the TC. **Queue Villagers non-stop** and don't stop until the game's over.
> Drop the other sheep nearby so they're ready.

**`4` Sheep** — Build a **House**, then onto sheep.

**`5` Sheep** — Build a **House**, then onto sheep. *(6 on food)*

**`6` Wood** — Build a **Lumber Camp** (~2:00) and start chopping. ◄ *your #1 fix — in your losses this came at 6:23*

**`7` Boar** — **Lure the boar** and eat it under the TC. *(≈340 free food — this is what keeps your TC from going idle)*

**`8` Wood** — To the Lumber Camp. *(2 on wood)*

**`9–10` Wood** — Lumber Camp. *(4 on wood — enough for the Barracks + Feudal)*

**`11` Boar** — **Lure the second boar.**

**`12` Berries** — Build a **Mill** on berries (~4:20), gather berries.

**`13–14` Food** — Berries / sheep, whatever's closest. Keep that TC working.

**`15` Barracks** — Build a **Barracks** in the **Dark Age** (~7:30–8:00), then back to food. ◄ *the whole rush lives here — not minute 14!*

**`16–18` Food/Wood** — Balance to afford Feudal. Around **8:30, queue `3` Militia** from the Barracks.

> **⏫ ▶ Click Feudal at ~18 pop (~9:45).** Your 3 Militia walk out as you age up.

### ⚔️ Feudal Age — *the rush*

**Research `Man-at-Arms`** the *instant* you hit Feudal — Goth discount, it's cheap. Add **Loom** if you skipped it.

**Send the army in.** Walk Militia → MAA into his villager line. Goal: **make vils run, force walls, snipe stragglers.** Don't suicide into the TC or Spears for one kill — *disruption > trades.*

**`2` Farms** — Start the food transition as the sheep run dry.

**Build `Blacksmith` → Forging** so your infantry actually bite.

**Keep the Barracks flowing** — feed Militia waves (you did 5-at-once around 14:30). Add **Double-Bit Axe** for wood.

### 🔄 Reading the enemy — *how you flip the script*

This is the part that turns "he pressures me and I suffer" into the opposite. **React to what he builds:**

- **He opens Scouts** (Stable ~10:00 — shura's bread and butter) → add **Spearmen**. Goth Spears
  are dirt cheap and **hard-counter cavalry** — his raiding Scouts walk into them and *die*. Now
  he can't touch your eco, while your **MAA keep eating his.** That's the whole flip.
- **His Archers come** (~16:00) → Archers beat your MAA and Spears. Answer with **Skirmishers**
  (they shut down archers) **or** just be in **Castle Age** already with the boom rolling.
- **Your damage core stays MAA** (+ a few of your own Archers if you added a Range). The Spears
  and Skirms are *defense that lets the MAA keep working* — they're not the killers, the MAA are.

> One-liner: **MAA do the damage, Spears delete his Scouts, Skirms cover his Archers, and you
> keep aging.** He's used to bullying you with Scouts — make those Scouts the worst unit he owns.

### 🏰 Decision point (~16–17 min)

- **Rush is working** (vils dying, he's walling, his eco stalled) → keep poking, but **start your boom in parallel.**
- **Rush whiffed** (he's stable, you're trading into Spears) → **STOP. Go Castle.** ◄ *in your win you sat in Feudal until 25:28 — that's the one thing to fix.*

Goths spike *late*. The rush buys you the time and map space to reach a safe **3-TC boom → Castle → Imperial**, where the cheap-infantry death-ball actually wins.

## Tips

- **Never let the Town Center sit empty.** Your idle TC is your single biggest leak — luring both
  boars is what guarantees you can always afford the next villager.
- **Lumber Camp by ~2:00.** This one timing is the difference between your wins and your losses.
- **No Scouts.** Goths have zero cavalry bonus; a stable of Scouts just delays your boom. Aggression
  as Goths = **Men-at-Arms**, full stop.
- **3 Town Centers max** in the boom that follows — spend the surplus on Barracks and army, not TCs.
